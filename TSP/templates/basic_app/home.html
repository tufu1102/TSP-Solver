Welcome to Travelling Sales Man Solver App!



The Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP) is a well-known problem in theoretical computer science and operations research. The standard version of the TSP is a hard problem and belongs to the NP-Hard class. In this project, we build an application to implement the TSP by the DP approach and the NN approach.

Select an approach from the navbar

- Choose your input type.
- Enter/Upload appropriate input.
- Our algorithms solve the TSP and generate a real-world route map for you.
